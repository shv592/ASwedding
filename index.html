Haldi & Devgon
10:00 am – 2:00 pm

A joyful pre-wedding celebration where turmeric paste (Haldi) is applied to the bride and groom as a blessing for prosperity. This is followed by Devgon, a traditional Kashmiri puja for the bride where she is donned in new clothes in preparation for the wedding. Lunch will be served.

FAQ
When is the RSVP deadline? +
Please RSVP by April 15th so we can have an accurate headcount. 🙂
What will the weather be like? +
August in Vaughan is warm and summery — 22–28°C (72–82°F). Evening events are indoors; morning events are outdoors, so pack light layers and breathable fabrics.
What should I wear? +
Indian events (Haldi, Mehndi, Sangeet): Colourful and festive — sarees, lehengas, sherwanis, kurtas!

Reception (August 22 evening): Black Tie Formal — gowns, tuxes, formal suits.
Is the wedding indoors or outdoors? +
Haldi and Lagan are outdoors. Sangeet and Reception are indoors.
Where should I park? +
There is plenty of free parking at the venue.
Is transportation provided between the reception and hotels? +
Uber, Lyft, and taxis are easily available in Vaughan and the GTA.
What can I do between the ceremony and reception? +
Return to your hotel to rest and change, or stay at the venue to relax and socialize.
Where should I stay? +
We recommend the Aloft Hotel — closest to the venue. Use discount code W14. See the Travel section for more details.
